What our product contains


Our ceramic version is clay molded to make beautiful designs. We have a ring pot, triple pot, coil pot, and a book pot for the ceramic versions. We also paint the ceramic pots so that they can be beautiful and admired by everyone. Our Deluxe Especial contains colored sand around the succuelnt to make beautiful patterns everyone will love. We have the succulent in a foil pot so that the sand and soil to not mix together and harm the succulent, so do not worry. We have a glass pot around the colored sand so that these fantastic patterns can be seen and admired.
